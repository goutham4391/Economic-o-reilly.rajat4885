About us

Inspiring the future for more than 40 years

O’Reilly’s mission is to change the world by sharing the knowledge of innovators. For over 40 years, we’ve inspired companies and individuals to do new things—and do things better—by providing them with the skills and understanding that are necessary for success.

At the heart of our business is a unique network of experts and innovators who share their knowledge through us. Our renowned animal books have been heralded for decades as the definitive place to learn about the technologies that are shaping the future. And the O’Reilly learning platform offers exclusive live events, interactive learning, a certification experience, books, videos, and more, making it easier for our customers to develop the expertise they need to get ahead. Everything we do is to help professionals from a variety of fields learn best practices and discover emerging trends that will shape the future of their industries.

Our customers are hungry to build the innovations that propel the world forward. And we help you do just that.

Tim O’Reilly

Tim has a history of convening conversations that reshape the computer industry. If you’ve heard the term “open source software” or “Web 2.0,” he’s had a hand in framing each of those big ideas. He’s the founder, CEO, and chairman of O’Reilly.
