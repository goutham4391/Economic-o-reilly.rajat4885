Reseller partner program

The pace of change in business is fast, accelerating, and unrelenting. Providing employees with training and the ability to get answers quickly is essential, not only to stay competitive but to successfully roll out mission-critical business objectives, such as cloud deployments, application development projects, and the implementation of emerging technologies.

With O’Reilly, your team has unlimited access to highly trusted, highly technical content—plus resources on business transformation, soft skills like communication, and more. It’s all developed or handpicked by our in-house team of editorial experts and tailored to all levels, from beginners to experts and everywhere in between.

That’s why over 2.8 million users, 5,000 enterprise companies, and more than 60% of the Fortune 100 depend on O’Reilly for their professional learning needs.

Become an O’Reilly reseller

As an O’Reilly reseller, you’ll be able to resell or bundle the award-winning O’Reilly learning platform to complement your own products and services. The O’Reilly learning platform will provide or augment the training necessary for successful implementation of new technological tools—plus you’ll help your clients upskill their employees company-wide and become more competitive in hiring and retaining talent.

Partner with O’Reilly to:

  • Offer a learning solution backed by the stellar reputation O’Reilly has built over 40 years as a trusted source of high-quality information on technology and business
  • Increase the value of your products and services
  • Accelerate and boost satisfaction with new technology implementations
  • Earn recurring revenue with client renewals
