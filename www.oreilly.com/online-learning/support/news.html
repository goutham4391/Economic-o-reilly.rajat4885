July 2022

Dog days of summer? Not for O’Reilly’s developers. Take a look at what’s new on the O’Reilly learning platform.

Cloud labs (for enterprise and teams)

It’s official. We’ve got over 80 new Azure cloud labs (and more on the way). For accounts with access to our interactive learning content, cloud labs join our lineup of tools you can use to get hands-on practice—including labs (formerly scenarios) to build new skills with step-by-step guidance, challenges to apply those skills with no guidance (just hints), and sandboxes to experiment in a safe environment.

Reader enhancements

The reader is where the learning happens, so we’re always looking to improve it. You can now pin the table of contents and keep it open while you scroll through a book. Plus, chapters and subsections in the table of contents are now condensed and expandable for quicker navigation. And it’s even easier to add, edit, and delete highlights as you read.

October 2021

Sit down with a pumpkin-spice latte and check out all the latest features on O’Reilly. You’re sure to find several ways to save time and help you finish 2021 on a high note.

Reader and video player

A new unified content experience across books, videos, and learning paths offers a ton of new features and improvements to explore.

  • Books now give you detailed progress monitoring for the entire book (and for each chapter) as well as the ability to add the book or chapter to a playlist.
  • Videos and learning paths have a new picture-in-picture functionality that lets you watch any video while working in other tabs or windows. You can follow along in a live sandbox environment or ask O’Reilly Answers about something you’ve just heard.
Find a book Find a video Find a learning path

Translated closed captions

All English-language O’Reilly videos now offer translated closed captioning in Japanese, Traditional Chinese (Taiwanese), Simplified Chinese, Korean, French, German, and Spanish. Simply click the globe icon in the video player to select a language and choose other captions settings.

Try now

Answers from video

With O’Reilly Answers, you can already get quick, specific answers to your questions in the form of snippets of code and highlighted passages from books. But now you’ll also get answers through video courses, popular Superstream online tech conferences, and case studies.

Get answers

Mobile app updates

Have an iOS mobile device? You now have the option to scroll or swipe through pages. Just select Scroll Mode or Swipe Mode on the toggle in the reader settings. And the Android app is lighter and brighter, with a more modern aesthetic and improved legibility. (Coming soon to iOS.)

New live events features

Quickly browse all events, jump into events you’ve registered for, and find recordings from your past events with a new tabbed experience. And you can now filter events by the time of day so you can easily fit learning into your schedule.
