What are the O’Reilly apps, and how do I download them?

Mobile app

The O’Reilly mobile app gives you unlimited access to books, videos, live online events and courses, playlists, and learning paths on the go, and it’s where you can download content for offline viewing. Available for iOS in the Apple App Store and Android on Google Play and Amazon.

TV apps

Our Roku and Amazon Fire TV apps let you watch all O’Reilly videos, Superstream events, and conversations with experts on the big screen. You can download them from the Roku Channel Store and Amazon.

Chrome extension (beta)

The Chrome browser extension allows you to jump back into your content or discover something new when you open a new tab. And you get quick access to trusted O’Reilly Answers when searching with Google or Stack Overflow. Download it from the Chrome Web Store.

Can I watch a live event on the mobile app?

Yes, you can watch a live event on the mobile app, but you’ll have to register for it in advance on your desktop.

Can I use O’Reilly on my Kindle?

Most e-readers, including Amazon’s Kindle devices, prevent the installation of third-party applications. Due to the nature of our business, we can only distribute content through our own apps—for more or less the same reason that Spotify songs can’t be played in iTunes, for example.

Can I sideload the O’Reilly app onto my Android device or Amazon tablet?

If your Android device is unable to connect to Google Play or the Amazon Appstore but otherwise meets the requirements for installation, you can download an APK for sideloading onto your device from this link. Please note that sideloaded apps don’t automatically receive updates, so you’ll need to keep track of that yourself.
